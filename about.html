About

I'm a 20-year-old student at HVA, pursuing a degree in Communication and Multimedia.

Before this, I graduated from Jan des Bouvrie College, where I developed my passion for design. If you have any questions or would like to connect, feel free to reach out!

  • Interior Design

    I design functional and beautiful spaces, using Procreate for sketching concepts and Vectorworks for precise 2D and 3D plans. These tools allow me to transform ideas into immersive environments that inspire and elevate everyday living.

  • Digital Design

    I craft engaging digital experiences, using Figma for collaborative design and Adobe tools for detailed visuals and branding. These platforms help me create cohesive, intuitive designs that captivate and connect with users.

  • Front-end Development

    I bring designs to life through HTML and CSS, creating responsive and visually appealing websites. While I'm fluent in these languages, I'm continuously expanding my skills in JavaScript to add more interactivity and dynamic functionality to my projects.

  • 3D Design

    I use Blender to create simple, aesthetic 3D designs that focus on clean lines and impactful visuals. My approach emphasizes minimalist forms, allowing the beauty of simplicity to shine through in each project.
