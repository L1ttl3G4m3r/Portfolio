Hi, I'm Kaylee, and welcome to my portfolio! Join me on a journey as I share how I've reached this point in my career. If you're curious to learn more about me, scroll down and explore!

  • My journey began with a love for sharing my creativity with the world. Studying interior design taught me the art of blending functionality with beauty to create environments that truly resonate. However, I soon realized my creativity extended beyond physical spaces, leading me to explore new possibilities.

  • As I delved into digital design, I became fascinated by the power of virtual spaces to captivate users just like physical environments do. Experimenting with graphic design and UI/UX introduced me to a new way of thinking about user experience. It was then that I knew digital design was where I wanted to grow.

To further pursue digital design, I began formal studies while also exploring front-end development on the side. Learning to code allowed me to merge my design skills with technical implementation, creating dynamic, interactive experiences. This combination of creativity and technology continues to fuel my passion for design today.

Each step of my journey has taught me that design, whether physical or digital, is about understanding people and creating solutions that enrich their experiences.
