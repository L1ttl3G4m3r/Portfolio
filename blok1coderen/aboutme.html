About Me Why? Contact

  1. My name is Kaylee Hoek and I currently (2023) am studying at the HVA in Amsterdam, the Netherlands. I have a few hobbies that I like to share with you.

    One of my most practised hobbies would be playing the ukulele. I have a keyboard and harmonica aswell, but I don't really play them that well. I love making music as well as improvising and making something of my own. It gives me motivation to keep on following my dreams.

    Another one of my big hobbies/lifestyle would be that I adore my dog Baloo. I care for him most of the time as well as: feed him, walk him, cuddle him and care for him. He is my buddy for life and because of him I believe "everyone can be kind to you as long as you keep giving."

    I am a huge fan of campfire and coziness. If I could stay under a blanket all day with a warm cup of tea and a fire burning next to me, I definitly will. I currently own about three blankets and I use then on a almost daily basis (Ofcourse I don't use any in the summer). You will probably catch me with hoodies on aswell.

    And last but not least. I love to play games. Not only video games, but also games like: Jenga, Cards against Humanity, Monopoly, The Game of Life and so on. If you want to keep me awake then the best way to do so is playing these games with me.

  2. The whole reason why I made this website is, because of the games that I played on my desktop. I feel like I have played so many games in my life that I wanted to start out with making my own game library.

    Now I do think the purpose of a website is for other people to see it. So making a game library for myself would benefit no one.

    That's how I came up with the idea of leaving my own review of the games that I played so that if you wanted to check out any of the games that I played, you will get a honest review + a star rating to help you make a good decision of your own.

    I know that my review alone would not be as interesting as having multiple reviews. That is also why I would like you to send me your opinion and help me set up this website for you all.

    This information is something I will consider in the reviews of the game, making it interesting and accurate. Maybe your quote will also be in the review!
